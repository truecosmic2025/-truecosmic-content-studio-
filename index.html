How Your Team Uses This
A simple daily workflow

Simple daily workflow

The AI does the writing. Your team does the reviewing. What used to take hours now takes minutes for a week's worth of content.

01
Set up your voice
Each team member creates their Voice Profile once — describing how they write, the words they use, slang they're comfortable with, and phrases to avoid.
02
Paste article URLs
Collect this week's article URLs from Truecosmic.com. Paste them all at once, one per line. The tool handles the rest.
03
Generate & review
Hit Generate. The AI reads each article, grabs the header image, and writes a post in your exact voice. Review each one and tweak if needed.
04
Schedule & comment
Copy the post body into Facebook. Schedule it natively in the group. The moment it publishes, paste the article link as the very first comment.

Why link in comments?

Facebook's algorithm actively suppresses posts with external links in the body. Putting the link as the first comment bypasses this and protects your reach. This is the single most important thing your team does.

Image tip

The tool pulls the article's header image automatically. When you post in Facebook, attach this image to the post body — images with no link in the post body get boosted reach.

Post frequency

Even with a 95k group, 1–2 posts per day is the sweet spot. More than that and Facebook starts throttling the group's organic reach. Spread your 100+ articles over weeks, not days.
